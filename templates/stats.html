How We Calculate This

Waste percentages are derived from EPA, USDA, and company sustainability reports. "Families fed" uses USDA data for a family of 4 (38 lbs/week). We always round down. We never inflate.

Subsidy data is sourced from USDA procurement records, EWG Farm Subsidy Database, and public SEC filings. All sources are public record.

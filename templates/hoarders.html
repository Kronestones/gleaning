Families Fed — Methodology

We calculate how many families could have been fed using USDA data for a family of 4 (2 adults, 2 children):

· 5.5 lbs per day  |  38 lbs per week  |  2,000 lbs per year

We divide documented waste by 38 lbs to calculate weekly family equivalents. We always round down. We never inflate. If the math doesn't produce a whole family, we don't count it.

This methodology is public and locked. It cannot be changed without founder review.

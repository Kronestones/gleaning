Food is a human right. This is a record, not a directive. Project Gleaning documents edible food wasted by businesses, witnessed by community members in their area. We are not responsible for any activities that occur as a result of information posted here.

Dumpster diving is legal in all 50 states under California v. Greenwood (1988). Once discarded, food is in the public domain. Know your local ordinances.

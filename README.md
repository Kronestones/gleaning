## What Gleaning Is

Gleaning is a free platform that connects food surplus
directly to the people who need it.

Three corporations own nearly every grocery store and
nearly every brand on their shelves. They give us the
illusion of choice while controlling the harvest.

Every day, tons of food are thrown away while people
go hungry in the same zip code.

That is not an accident. That is a choice.
Gleaning is the counter-choice.

## What Gleaning Does

- **The Map** — real time surplus visibility.
  What's being thrown away, where, right now.
  Businesses that participate are visible.
  Businesses that don't are visible too.

- **The Match** — surplus to pantry, direct.
  No middleman. No bureaucracy. No delay.
  Food posted. Need posted. Match made. Pickup arranged.

- **The Truth Wall** — who owns what.
  The illusion of choice, exposed.
  Forty brands. Three owners. Now you know.

## The Promise

We will never charge for this.
We will never sell your data.
We will tell the truth about who owns what.
We will make waste visible and silence damning.
We will connect surplus to need as fast as possible.
We will not pretend the system is broken — it works
exactly as designed. We are building the alternative.

The harvest was never only theirs.
